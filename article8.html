Skincare routine

Posted on: 10/03/19

Maybe two years ago I could say that I had an almost perfect skin. My face was smooth, I rarely used to get any blemishes and most of the time there really wasnâ€™t a big difference between not wearing and wearing foundation on my face as it was so clear. However, since then, especially in the past year, my skin has really changed in a negative way. Even in puberty, I never went through serious phases of skin problems and all I used to get was a spot here and there sometimes, so I was very used to having unproblematic skin. It seems like all of a sudden everything changed out of the blue. I started to get spots very often, most of them being the type that stays on your face for a month no matter how much you try to get rid of it and then leaves a darkened area afterwards, my pores got enlarged, some areas of my skin became increasingly oily whilst some of them went dry and I also started getting many white-/blackheads on my T-zone, cheeks and especially in front of my ears. Not taking care of my skin at all transformed into a process of trying out different products and hoping they will be effective. I decided to share with you my current little skincare routine, that I find to be having a good effect on my skin problems, in case you are experiencing something similar or are just curious (like me) about what other people use.

Firstly, most of the time I use this Neutrogena Visibly Clear moisturiser when I wake up and wash my face. It feels refreshing and I find that if I am to put makeup on afterwards, it helps with preventing foundation looking crazy over dry patches whilst still not emphasising oily areas on the other hand, even after hours of wear like some other moisturisers.

In the evening, after removing my makeup, if I am wearing any, I cleanse my face with the Neutrogena Visibly Clear facial wash to remove all the remaining makeup and/or dirt on my face. Then I use the Clean&Clear blackhead clearing scrub on my problem areas but although this one in particular says it can be used daily, I try to use it every other day instead, as otherwise my skin starts to feel a bit irritated after a while.

After this, I use Neutrogena Visibly Clear cleansing lotion.
