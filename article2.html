What smoothie I make myself in the morning

Posted on: 12/02/19

For half a year now, I’ve been drinking smoothies at least twice a week. There are even periods of time when I have them for three/four days in a row and to be fair the days in which I don’t have a smoothie are days when I have run out of products or I am just too lazy to make one. I do also sometimes drink an Innocent or Naked but having them regularly is too expensive and by now homemade smoothies have started to taste just as good if not better at times!

Me and my mum got into smoothies together and started getting them regularly from the store. It was not long before we also decided to start making them ourselves. At first I was using normal milk with the fruits, which tastes really nice but ends up being more like a milkshake rather than a smoothie. Then we tried switching it up to mineral water which I wasn’t very keen on until I got used to it. I found that it also depends on what fruits you use. Generally, with plain water, the more fruits – the better. Otherwise, I guess it could be found a bit weird in taste. Also, having berries in there helps a lot by adding ‘texture’.

Shortly after placing the start of my little obsession with smoothies, I started putting powder food supplements in them. These Funktional Foods powders sounded and looked very nice as well as all said on their packaging that they could be used in smoothies. Reducing tiredness and fatigue and helping the immune system. Great! The spirulina one didn’t taste very nice but it claimed to be aiding my immune system so I kept putting it in. It probably took me around two weeks though, before I figured out that having these didn’t make me feel very well. I still don’t know if it was just one of these that my body didn’t work well with or if it was my mistake of mixing them all together so I still haven’t made myself retry them, but if you are someone who feels like you need to provide your body with something extra, definitely try one of these or just something similar and see how you’re finding it. You never know until you try.

Of course, everyone has his own taste and likes different ingredients in a smoothie but I can’t help but share with you my personal favourite set of ingredients for the perfect smoothie! These are:

• Alpro coconut and almond
• Freshly squeezed apple and carrot
• Banana
• Strawberry
• Exotic fruits
