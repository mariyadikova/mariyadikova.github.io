Home remedies I know from my grandmother

Posted on: 29/12/18

DIY beauty hacks and treatments are very helpful and everyone knows that. Here are three home ‘remedies’ I know from my grandmother that I have tried many times before and I find really useful:

Sodium bicarbonate for whiter teeth – Dusting some sodium bicarbonate over your toothbrush after you have washed your teeth in the evening and gently rubbing in your teeth is a very useful hack for whitening your teeth. You should be aware though, that this type of soda can cause irritation to your teeth and gums so you should not do it for a very long period of time.

Egg yolk for the face – Egg yolk is a natural skin hydrator and that’s why it is so beneficial, especially for dry skin types. Applying an egg yolk over your face after you have washed it and leaving it on for half an hour every week is guaranteed to make your face way more soft and moisturised.

Vinegar for oily scalp/hair – A bowl of water mixed with 2 table spoons of vinegar are a handy and cheap method to battle oily hair or just make your wash more long-lasting. After washing your hair, you can pour this mixture on your head and try and get it in your scalp as much as you can. Don’t rinse afterwards. Your hair is not supposed to smell like vinegar as you haven’t used much of it but if you feel like you can catch any unusual smell – don’t worry - it is going to evaporate soon.
