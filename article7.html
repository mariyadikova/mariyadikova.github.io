Uses of yoghurt

Posted on: 22/01/19

Another product that you might have by now found out that is strongly used in Bulgarian foods is yoghurt. Not just any yoghurt though, but Bulgarian yoghurt. Here in the UK, Greek style yoghurt is the one closest to it but it is still not quite the same. Lactobacillus bulgaricus, the bacteria that curdles milk, is named after Bulgaria, so I can proudly say that my country is the birthplace of this amazing product.
