A few home decorations that have been making me feel very cosy lately

Posted on: 08/12/18

I recently got myself my own personal study/work room to do all my work in, instead of my bedroom which used to be very uncomfortable as I didn’t have space for a desk and had to do everything on my bed. I am very happy with how it turned out and although it is still not quite finished and there are more pieces of furniture that I need to get in there, it’s my own little personal space and makes me feel quite motivated.

I don’t know if it came with aging up or it’s just a personality thing, but having everything sorted out and tidy around me when I need to do work has become of a massive importance to me. I need peace and quiet and everything in its place, otherwise I get frustrated and distracted. Having objects around me that make me feel cosy and relaxed is also very much appreciated. Here are some suggestions I have for things that you can place near your ‘working place’ at home that might make you feel nice and homey.

Whilst artificial flowers are in no doubt practical, I find having a fresh set of flowers around me really uplifting. Plus, they sometimes smell nice!

Candles, candles, candles! … And fairylights… in a dark room. What more could you say? It’s one of the nicest feelings.
