About Me & the Blog

Hello and welcome to my blog! My name is Maria and I am 21 years old. I am born in Bulgaria but I have been living in the UK for 5 years now. I study Media & Communications at Goldsmiths, University of London and I'm soon to be a graduate.

More...

When I was a lot younger, I used to have many hobbies and things that I could say I was good at - things that I later dropped off and abandoned for one reason or another. Writing was one of those things. I enjoyed writing essays for school or just making up my own stories, pretending I was an author. I even had a dream of being a journalist at one point in my life.

My personal reason behind this blog lies in trying to reconnect with one of the things that I used to be passionate about and that is writing.

In this blog you will be able to read mainly about lifestyle and beauty original content, that is going to feature my own findings, tastes, recommendations, opinions and tips, as well as some of my personal stories and experience.

Whether this blog and the information I provide would be of any help to you or would just simply act as a source of entertainment and relaxation, I hope you enjoy it!

With love sweeter than honey,
Maria
